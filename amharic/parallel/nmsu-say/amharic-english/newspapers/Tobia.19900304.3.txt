

                   ሕፃናት በአፍ መፍቻ ቋንቋቸው እንዳይማሩ እንቅፋት በዝቷል

       There have been obstacles not to teach lessons to children in their mother tongue   

  በኦሮሚያ ክልላዊ መስተዳድር በሚገኙ ዞኖች ያሉ ኦሮሞ ያልሆኑ ሕፃናት ከ1990 ዓ.ም የትምህርት ዘመን ጀምሮ በአፍ መፍቻ ቋንቋቸው አማርኛ እንዲማሩ የተላለፈው ውሳኔ በደብረዘይት፣ በናዝሬትና በምሥራቅ ሐረርጌ ሥራ ላይ እንዳይውል የመስተዳድርና የትምህርት መምሪያዎች መሰናክል እየፈጠሩ መሆናቸው ታውቋል

  ይኸው የተባበሩት መንግሥታት ድርጅት ያሳለፈው ዓለም አቀፍ ድንጋጌና የኢትዮጵያ ፌደራላዊ ዴሞክራሲያዊ ሪፐብሊክ መንግሥትም በሕገ-መንግሥቱ ያፀደቀው ውሳኔ በሥራ እንዳይተረጐም ባለሥልጣናቱ የአማርኛ የትምህርት መጻሕፍት ውሰት እንዳይፈጸምና መምህራንም እንዳያስተምሩ በማስጠንቀቅ የሕፃናቱን ወርቃማ ጊዜ እያሳለፉ መሆናቸውን ከየአካባቢዎቹ የደረሱን ዘገባዎች ጠቁመዋል

  በምስራቅ ሸዋ ዞን በናዝሬት ከተማ በሚገኙ አስራ ሁለት 1ኛና መለስተኛ 2ኛ ደረጃ ት/ቤቶች በአማርኛ ተናጋሪ ህፃናት ላይ እየተፈፀመ ያለው ስውር ደባ ከሁሉም የከፋ መሆኑን የጠቀሱት ምንጮች በከተማው የሚታየው ሁኔታ የሌሎች ቋንቋዎች ተናጋሪ ተማሪዎችን በአንድ ቋንቋ ውጦ የማስቀረት አዝማሚያ እንዳለው ታውቋል

  በህፃናቱ ላይ እየተፈፀመ ያለው ዝርዝር ሁኔታ ከዚህ በፊት አፉን ባልፈታበት ቋንቋና ባልተለመደ የላቲን ፊደል፣ በሙያው ባልሰለጠኑ መምህራን ያለ ውድ በግድ የጨቅላ አእምሮአቸውን በማስጨነቅ ተመልሶ የማያገኙት ወርቃማ ጊዜአቸው በከንቱ እንዲያልፍ ማድረጉን ምንጮቹ ጠቅሰዋል

  በ1989 ዓ.ም በወላጆች ከፍተኛ ጥረትና በብዙ ውጣ ውረድ ህፃናት በአፍ መፍቻ ቋንቋቸው ይማሩ ዘንድ ከበላይ አካል ካስወሰኑም በኋላ በዞኑ ትምህርት መምሪያና በአስተዳደር በኩል ተግባራዊ እንዳይሆንና እንዲያውም እንደ ፖለቲካ ታይቶ ይህን ሃሣብ በሚከታተሉ ግለሰቦች ላይ በግልፅም ሆነ በስውር ችግር እንዲፈጠር መደረጉ ተመልክቷል

The decision to teach Non-Oromo children, who live in zones of Oromia regional administration in their native languages, Amharic, as from 1998 academic year onwards have faced obstacles by administrative and educational departments in Debrezeit, Nazareth, and eastern Harerghe, it was disclosed;

This international decree which the United Nations passed on and which the Ethiopian Federal Democratic Republic government also ratified it in its constitution, the authorities didn't want to implement it disallowing the borrowing of texts written in Amharic language, warning teachers not to teach, they are just wasting the children's golden time, according to the reports that reached us;

In 12 primary & junior secondary schools that are found in eastern shoa zone, Nazareth Town, the hidden intrigue done on children of Amharic speakers has become the worst of all, the sources mentioned, the situation that is seen in the town seems to have the inclination to suppress students who have other native languages learn in one Language;

The details of the intrigue done on the children are that attempting to teach them with a language they don't speak as a mother tongue before, with a Latin letter, with teachers who didn't take training, unwillingly through worry and great difficulty, just wasting the children's golden time, the sources disclosed;     

Even if in 1997 through a lot of efforts made by parents, and through so many ups and downs, they have made higher officials decide that children learn with their native language, the zonal education department and on the administrative sides this was made not practical, they even started to consider it political upon those individuals who follow these and openly or indirectly problems were made to occur on them, it was disclosed;

Today, it is not with the language the parents chose for their kinds that they learn, but students are assigned at any school where the authorities chose for them, without the consent of parents.

በዞኑ ያሉ የሚመለከታቸው የትምህርት ክፍል ኃላፊዎች አንዳችም ለትምህርቱ ቅድመ ዝግጅት ሳያደርጉ የ1990 ዓ.ም የትምህርት ዘመንም ተጀምሯል

  በናዝሬት ቁ.3 1ኛና መለስተኛ 2ኛ ደረጃ ት/ቤት በዓመቱ መጀመሪያ ላይ የት/ቤቱ ርዕሰ መምህር አቶ ዘውዱ ደበላ የት/ቤቱን መምህራን ሰብስበው ከአራተኛና ስምንተኛ ክፍሎች በስተቀር የማስተማሪያ መጻሕፍት ስለሌለ በሌሎች ክፍሎች የሚገቡ መምህራን ክፍል ገብቶ ፀጥታ ከመጠበቅ በስተቀር የቀድሞ የአማርኛ መጻሕፍትን ማስተማር እንደማይገባ ካስጠነቀቁ በኋላ የንብረት ክፍሏ መጻሕፍቱን እንዳታውስ ማገዳቸው ታውቋል

  በዚህ ዓይነት የአገር ተተኪ ዜጋ የሆኑት ህፃናት አፍላ የወጣትነት ጊዜያቸው በከንቱ እየተቃጠለ፣ መምህራንም አምራች ዜጋን ማፍራት ሲገባቸው ያገር ሸክም እንዲሆኑ ሳይወዱ በግድ እያደረጉአቸው መሆኑን ምንጮቹ አስረድተዋል

  ይህ በእንዲህ እንዳለም በአዳ ሊበን ወረዳ በደብረዘይት ከተማ የሚገኙ ወላጆችና ሀገር ወዳድ ሽማግሌዎች ሕፃናትን አፍ ባልፈቱበት ቋንቋ እንዲማሩ ማድረግ ማኅበራዊ እድገታቸውን መጐተት መሆኑን በማስረዳት ለበላይ አካላት ያቀረቡት አቤቱታ በተመሳሳይ ሁኔታ ተቀባይነትን ቢያገኝም አስፈፃሚዎች ውሳኔውን ለመቀልበስ በመሯሯጥ ላይ መሆናቸውን የደረሰን ዜና አመልክቷል

  በደብረዘይት ከተማ ነሐሴ 26 ቀን 1989 ዓ.ም በአፄ ልብነ ድንግል ት/ቤት አዳራሽ የኦሮሞ መምህራን፣ የት/ቤት ወላጅ ኮሚቴ አባላትና ሐሳቡን ይቃወማሉ ተብለው የተመረጡ የኦሮሞ ብሔረሰብ ተወላጆች በተገኙበት በተካሄደው ስብሰባ ይኸው ደባ በገሃድ መንፀባረቁን የጠቀሰው ዘገባ የመስተዳድሩና የትምህርት መምሪያ ኃላፊዎች በአማርኛ ቋንቋ የሚማር ሥራ ማግኘት እንደማይችል እስከማስጠንቀቅ መድረሳቸውን ገልጦአል

  የተባበሩት መንግሥታት ድርጅት ካሳለፈው ድንጋጌና የሀገሪቱም መንግሥት ካፀደቀው ሕገ መንግሥት ውጪ የስድስትና የስምንት ዓመት ሕፃናት የራሳቸውን እድል እራሳቸው ይወስኑ ተብሎ አማርኛ ወይም ኦሮምኛ እማራለሁ እያሉ እንዲመርጡና እንዲፈርሙ መደረጉ አግባብ ያለመሆኑን የጠቀሰው ዘገባ ሕዝቡ በንቃትና በስሜት ስብሰባውን ተከታትሎ ስለተቋቋማቸው ከብዙ ጭቅጭቅና ክርክር በኋላ ወላጆችና ሕፃናት በምዝገባው ይወስኑ ተብሎ በተሰጠው ውሳኔ መሠረት ምዝገባው ቀጥሎ አንዳንድ የአሻጥር ሥራ ቢሠራም ውጤት መገኘቱን አመልክቷል

  ውጤቱ በሰንጠረዥ ይህን ይመስላል ትምህርቱ ከተጀመረ በኋላ ወላጆች ልጆቻቸውን ት/ቤት ለማስገባት ሲሄዱ ግን «ለአማርኛ ቦታ የለንም።

Without making any preparations for the lesson by the pertinent zonal education department officials, the 1998 academic year has started;

In Nazareth No.3 primary and junior secondary school, Mr. Zewdu Debela, the principal of the school at the beginning of the school gathered the school teachers told them that except fourth and eight grade, there' wasn't any teaching books so teachers getting in to other classes have no other work except controlling the students and warned them not to use the former books written in Amharic and forbade the store keeper, to lend these books;

In this way, the children, who are the citizens that would be replacing today' generation, their early youth time is wasted in futility; while teachers, instead of producing productive citizens, are making them unwillingly, burdens of a country, the sources disclosed; 

In the mean time, parents in Ada Liben district, Debrezeit town and county-loving elderly people appealed to higher authorities and similarly got acceptance, but the implementing bodies are striving to avert the situation, the report which reached us indicated; 

In a meeting held in Debrezeit town on September 4, 1997 at the Hall of Emperor Lebnedengel School, teachers of Oromifa, parents board members an selected Oromo nationals who were expected to protest the proposed idea, participated but this intrigue was exposed, according to the report, the administrative and education bureau officials warned that those who learned in Amharic wouldn't get jobs;

Out of the decree of the United Nations and the constitution that the government approved, saying that children of six and eight should decide the language they would be learning and making them sign was just wrong, said the report, but due to the people's cautious and active participation in the meeting, after a long and tiresome dispute and argument it was decided that parents and children should choose the language, though not without some conspiracy, a fruit was gotten at last, it disclosed;

The oromifa language that has been given from 1st – 6th grade up to now, is told to be given to higher grades starting from 7th grade from this year on wards in different districts and towns of eastern Harerge, which made many non-Oromo speakers to leave their education, our eastern Ethiopia reporter disclosed; although parents who have the financial capability were able to resolve the problem by sending their kinds to Amharic-speaking schools, parents who have poor, small and meager income had no choice, so they either forced their kinds to learn with the language they don't know or keep them to home, the reporter disclosed quoting the parents;      

  አቅሙና ችሎታው ያላቸው ወላጆች ልጆቻቸውን የአማርኛ ቋንቋ ወደሚሰጥባቸው ት/ቤቶች በማስገባት ችግሩን ለመቋቋም ቢችሉም ድሆችና አነስተኛ ገቢያላቸው ወላጆች ግን አማራጭ ስለሌላቸው ልጆቻቸውን በማያውቁት ቋንቋ እንዲማሩ አለያም ከቤት ለማስቀረት መገደዳቸውን የገለጹለት መሆኑን አስታውቋል።
